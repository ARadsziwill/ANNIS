void annis.gui.visualizers.component.rst.RSTImpl.sortChildren ( JSONObject  root) throws JSONException
private

Sorts the children of root by the the sentence indizes.

Since the sentence indizes are based on the token indizes, some sentences have no sentences indizes, because sometimes token nodes are out of context.

A kind of insertion sort would be better than the used mergesort.

And it is a pity that the JSONArray has no interface to sort the underlying Array.
